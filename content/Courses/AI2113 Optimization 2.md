## Norm Approximation

Let $\mat{A}\in \mathbb{R}^{m\times n}$, $\vct{b}\in\mathbb{R}^m$.
The objective is
$$
\min_{x\in\mathbb{R}^n} \|\mat{A}x-\vct{b}\|
$$
