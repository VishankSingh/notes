The mutual information between two discrete random variables $X$ and $Y$ with joint distribution $p$ is defined as
$$
\mathbb{I}(X; Y) = \mathcal{D}_{KL}(p(X, Y) \| p(X) p(Y)) = \mathbb{H}(X) + \mathbb{H}(Y) - \mathbb{H}(X, Y)
$$
