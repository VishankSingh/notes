Let $X$ be any real valued random variable such that $a \leq X \leq b$ almost surely. Then for any $\lambda \in \mathbb{R}$,
$$
\mathbb{E}\left[e^{\lambda\left(X - \mathbb{E}[X]\right)}\right] \leq e^{\left(\frac{\lambda^2(b-a)^2}{8}\right)}
$$

Proof using convexity of $e^{\lambda x}$ and then AMGM inequality.
